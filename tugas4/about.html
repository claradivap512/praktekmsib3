Scoops Ahoy is an ice cream parlor that has been established since 1985. Located in the Food Court of Starcourt Mall--now Hawkins Mall--
Scoops Ahoy become a legend in offering unique ice cream flavors. Back then, there was only ranging options of ice cream. But due to popular
demand, our team decided that we should also include cookies, desserts, and sundae.
